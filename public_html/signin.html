How to Play:

  1. One player is designated as the guesser.
  2. All other players find a somewhat to very obscure Wikipedia article and read a bit about it.
  3. An article will be chosen randomly and all players must convince the guesser that it's their article by describing what it's about.
  4. If it's not your article, you have to make something up!
  5. At least 3 players are required, but you can play with however many you want!
